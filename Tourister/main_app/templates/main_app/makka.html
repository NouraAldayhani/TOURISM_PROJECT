Makkah

Makkah is a city located in the western region of Saudi Arabia, and it is considered one of the holiest cities in Islam. It is the birthplace of the Prophet Muhammad and the site of the Kaaba, which is the most sacred site in Islam and the focal point of the annual Hajj pilgrimage.
